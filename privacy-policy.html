Privacy Policy for Movie Title Translator

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Overview

Movie Title Translator is a mobile application that helps users find translated movie titles in different languages. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Usage Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • Search queries (movie titles you search for)
  • Language selections (source and target languages)
  • App interactions (which features are used)

This data is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Data Sent to Third Parties

When you search for a movie, your search query is sent to The Movie Database (TMDB) API to retrieve movie information and translations. TMDB's privacy policy applies to their handling of this data: https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account or login credentials
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Payment information

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to Firebase's default retention policies. We do not maintain any separate database of user information.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
